The Alchemist is a master of arcane forces and forbidden magic, excelling as a ranged spellcaster specializing in burst damage and manipulation. Beginning as an experimental scholar blending science and magic, they evolve through disciplined elemental mastery, dark warlock pacts, and ultimately become hemomancers manipulating life force itself. The Alchemist line dominates through overwhelming spell power but requires careful positioning due to fragility.
